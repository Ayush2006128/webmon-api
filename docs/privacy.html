Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

1. Data Collection

To facilitate advanced research functionalities, Webmon processes user queries and contextual data. We collect minimal metadata required for service operations, including usage frequency, latency metrics, and integration IDs (such as Discord User or Server IDs).

2. Data Usage and Security

All queries processed by Webmon are transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption. While we log conversational traces for diagnostic purposes (see Section 3), we strictly do not use your proprietary research or private conversations to train underlying foundational AI models without explicit, opt-in consent.

3. Debugging and Diagnostics

We use LangSmith exclusively for debugging and monitoring Webmon's logic and performance. Traces of your interactions may be logged to help our developers identify and fix errors in the agent's reasoning. These logs are stored securely, are only accessible to our core engineering team, and are never used to train public AI models.

4. Authentication and Sensitive Information

While initially your authentication details are securely handled and not shared with the underlying AI models, users must exercise caution during conversational interactions. You must not share passwords, API keys, or other highly sensitive personal information with the agent after authentication is complete.

5. Data Sharing

We strictly prohibit the sale of user data. Information is only shared with trusted cloud infrastructure providers necessary to operate the service, or when required to comply with legal obligations.

6. Platform Expansion

As Webmon expands into dedicated Web and Mobile environments, any new data collection practices (e.g., account registration, cross-device syncing) will be governed by updated, transparent policies subject to user consent.

7. Your Rights

Users maintain the right to request access to, modification of, or complete deletion of their data from our active servers at any time.
